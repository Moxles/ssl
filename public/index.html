Check out the to the left of the URL. This denotes that the certificate has been verified and SSL is working hard to encrypt the connection. In some browsers it will even be green ( https://ssl.moxles.com).

You can actually click the and get more details on the certificate. In this case the certificate is not attached to any specific person or company, but is still verified. This means the domain and certificate have been identified as correctly belonging together. After all, you don't want a certificate for totallynotphishingyou.com being used for wellsfargo.com, now do we?
